I'm Prakash Mishra, a Cybersecurity Engineer who has spent the last several years immersed in the Microsoft Security stack — from email threat protection to identity and cloud security. My career began in frontline technical support, where I built deep troubleshooting instincts across Microsoft 365 workloads. That foundation evolved through escalation engineering, infrastructure leadership, and now a specialist role on Microsoft's Defender for Office 365 (MDO) support team.

Today I work at the intersection of email security, threat hunting, and incident response — investigating phishing campaigns, tuning anti-phishing and Safe Links/Safe Attachments policies, validating SPF/DKIM/DMARC configurations, and correlating signals across Microsoft Defender XDR to help organizations contain real-world threats before they spread.

Alongside Defender for Office 365, I bring hands-on experience across Microsoft Entra ID, Azure AD Connect, Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, Microsoft Teams, and Microsoft Intune — giving me a full-stack view of how identity, endpoint, and collaboration security come together inside the Microsoft 365 ecosystem. I'm currently expanding into Microsoft Sentinel and KQL-driven threat hunting, and building toward the SC-200, SC-300, SC-100, and AZ-500 certifications to formalize my path into security operations and architecture.

What drives me is the investigative side of security — pulling apart an incident, tracing the blast radius across Defender products, and turning a messy alert into a clear, defensible remediation story.
